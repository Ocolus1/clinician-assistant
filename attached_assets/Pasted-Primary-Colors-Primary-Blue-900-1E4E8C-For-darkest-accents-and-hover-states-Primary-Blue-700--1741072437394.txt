Usage Guidelines

Text Colors:

Use Gray 800 for primary text
Use Gray 600 for headings
Use Gray 500 for secondary or supporting text
Use Gray 400 for placeholder or disabled text
Always use white text on dark backgrounds


Button Colors:

Primary actions: Primary Blue 500 (background) with white text
Secondary actions: White background with Primary Blue 500 border and text
Tertiary actions: No background/border with Primary Blue 500 text
Disabled state: Gray 300 background with Gray 400 text


Form Elements:

Input borders: Gray 300
Focus states: Primary Blue 500
Error states: Error 500
Background: White or Gray 50


Cards and Containers:

Background: White
Borders: Gray 200
Shadows: 0px 4px 6px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)


Navigation:

Active: Primary Blue 500
Hover: Gray 100
Background: White
Borders: Gray 200
